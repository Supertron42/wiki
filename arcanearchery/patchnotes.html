Change Logs

Version history and release notes for Arcane Archery.

Newest releases appear at the top. Check back after updates to see what's changed.

Version 1.1.0 - Visual and Combat Fixes

Fixes

  • FIX 3D spell aura on drawn arrows. The glow around drawn spell-arrows is now a full 3D particle effect instead of a flat overlay.
  • FIX Aura persists through camera switch. Switching between first and third person no longer removes the spell glow from your arrows.
  • FIX Spell damage now hits NPCs. Spell-arrows now deal their full spell damage to NPCs and creatures.
  • FIX Consistent magicka cost. Magicka is now deducted reliably on every spell-arrow impact.

Version 1.0.0 - Initial Release

Features

  • NEW Bind spells to arrows. Equip a spell and arrows, press a hotkey, and every arrow you fire carries that spell.
  • NEW Spells trigger on impact. When a spell-bound arrow hits something, the spell goes off at the point of impact.
  • NEW Configurable magicka cost. Set how much magicka spell-arrows cost compared to normal casting (default: half cost).
  • NEW Living targets only mode. Optionally limit spell triggers to NPCs and creatures.
  • NEW NPC spell-arrows. Configure any NPC to fire spell-arrows through the INI file.
  • NEW MCM menu. All settings are adjustable in-game through SkyUI's Mod Configuration Menu.
  • NEW Persistent bindings. Spell-arrow bindings are saved with your game and survive load-order changes.
  • NEW SE, AE, and VR support. Works on all three Skyrim editions.
