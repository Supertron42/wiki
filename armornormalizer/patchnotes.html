Version 0.1.0 - Initial Release

Features

  • NEW Automatic stat normalization. Sets modded armor protection, gold value, and weight to vanilla standards based on detected material tier.
  • NEW Material detection from crafting recipes. Identifies each armor's material by analyzing which ingots its crafting recipe uses.
  • NEW Keyword fallback detection. If an armor has no crafting recipe, material keywords are used instead.
  • NEW Enchanted armor support. Enchanted items get their base stats normalized while keeping the enchantment value bonus.
  • NEW Crafting recipe normalization. Adjusts ingot and leather strip counts to match vanilla amounts without removing special ingredients.
  • NEW Per-mod crafting blacklist. Block any mod from adding items to your crafting menus.
  • NEW World distribution. Adds modded armors to merchant shops and loot containers at level-appropriate tiers.
  • NEW MCM configuration menu. Five pages of settings with multiplier sliders, feature toggles, per-mod blacklist, and live statistics.
  • NEW SE, AE, and VR support. Works on all three Skyrim editions.
