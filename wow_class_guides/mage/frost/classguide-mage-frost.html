WoW Classic Anniversary TBC - Frost Mage DPS Guide

Your fast track to a raid-ready Frost Mage in WoW Classic Anniversary (TBC). Pre-raid BiS, per-phase raid upgrades, gems, enchants, consumables, talents, and the max-DPS rotation, with every pick ranked by consensus across the major mage guides and checked against live item stats. Hover any item, boss, or ability for full Wowhead tooltips.

Overview

Frost is the safe, consistent ranged caster: rock-steady single-target damage from Frostbolt, a Water Elemental pet, and built-in survivability (snares, Frost Nova, Blink). It is not the top raid parser (Fire and Arcane out-scale it), but it is forgiving, great for PvP and farming, and brings real raid utility: the Winter's Chill debuff (stacking +crit to frost damage on the boss), Arcane Brilliance, sheep CC, and conjured food/water/mana gems. The whole build is about feeding Frostbolt and hitting one cap first:

1. Hit the spell hit cap. You need 16% spell hit (~202 hit rating) so spells stop missing raid bosses. With 3/3 Elemental Precision (the Arcane dip every frost build takes) that drops to 13% from gear (~164 rating). Hit is your #1 stat until capped.

2. Then follow the stat priority: Spell Hit (to cap) > Spell Damage > Spell Crit > Spell Haste > Intellect. Frost loves crit because of Shatter (guaranteed crits on frozen targets) and Winter's Chill.

Rotation & DPS

Frost is a simple, low-APM rotation: keep Frostbolt rolling, layer your cooldowns into haste windows, and manage mana so you never go dry. Built from the Icy Veins Frost Mage rotation guide.

The whole rotation in one line: cast Frostbolt on repeat; open with Summon Water Elemental + Icy Veins for a burst window; while moving use Fire Blast (or Ice Lance on a frozen target); keep Winter's Chill stacked on the boss.

Single-Target Priority

  1. Summon Water Elemental on cooldown. The pet adds free sustained damage, keep it out whenever it is up.
  2. Icy Veins on cooldown for a +20% spell-haste burst window (line it up with Bloodlust and trinkets).
  3. Frostbolt is your entire filler. Cast it back to back; it also stacks Winter's Chill on the target (up to 5 = +10% crit to frost spells for the raid).
  4. While moving: Fire Blast if the target is not frozen, or Ice Lance if it is (a guaranteed Shatter crit). Do not stand still casting nothing.

Don't clip Frostbolt. Let each cast finish; chasing procs or weaving extra buttons usually lowers DPS. Frost is about uptime, keep casting and minimise movement.

Multi-Target / AoE

  • Blizzard is your main AoE (talented Improved Blizzard also snares the pack).
  • Cone of Cold for tight clumps in melee range, and to proc freezes for Shatter.
  • Arcane Explosion if you are surrounded, but it is very mana-hungry; Flamestrike for stacked packs.
  • Frost Nova + Blink to root and peel away when kiting.

Cooldowns

CooldownEffectWhen to use
Icy Veins+20% spell haste for 20 secOn cooldown; stack with Bloodlust, trinkets and a potion for burst
Summon Water ElementalSummons a pet that nukes and can Freeze targetsKeep it out on cooldown; its Freeze sets up Shatter
Cold SnapInstantly resets all your Frost cooldownsUse Icy Veins + Water Elemental, then Cold Snap to do both again
Bloodlust / Heroism+30% party haste for 40 secShaman/raid cooldown; pool nothing, just cast through it

Race CDs stack into the burst window: Troll Berserking (haste); Blood Elf Arcane Torrent returns mana. Pop everything together, then again on cooldown.

Mana Management

  • Conjure a Mana Emerald before the pull and use the Mana Gem the moment you dip (it is free mana on a short cooldown).
  • Evocation on cooldown whenever it is safe to channel; it restores a big chunk of your mana bar.
  • Carry mana potions and a Dark/Demonic Rune and alternate them (separate cooldowns) on long fights.
  • Drink to full between pulls. Molten Armor is the DPS armor (crit); swap to Mage Armor only if a fight is a true mana marathon.

Keep These Up

  • Arcane Brilliance on the raid (or Arcane Intellect on yourself).
  • Your Molten Armor buff for the crit.
  • Five stacks of Winter's Chill on the boss (you apply it with Frostbolt; it helps every frost caster).

Talents

The raid build is deep Frost (about 10/0/51), dipping into Arcane for Elemental Precision (spell hit) and Arcane Concentration (free casts/mana). Open the Wowhead TBC talent calculator to build and export it.

The talents that define the spec:

  • Summon Water Elemental and Cold Snap, your big Frost cooldowns.
  • Empowered Frostbolt (more damage + crit on your main nuke) and Ice Shards (+crit damage).
  • Piercing Ice and Winter's Chill for raw frost damage and the raid crit debuff.
  • Shatter (guaranteed crits on frozen targets) and Icy Veins.
  • The Arcane dip grabs Elemental Precision (+3% hit) and Arcane Concentration (Clearcasting procs).

Questing Strategy

Quests reward caster gear, gold, rep, and XP all at once, but only if you batch them. Pick up everything at a hub before you start killing, and most slots in your pre-raid set come from solo questing.

Universal rule: when you reach a quest hub, walk every NPC with a yellow ! before doing anything. Leave town with a full log and a loop that finishes several quests at once. The gear below is a low-effort head start before you grind heroics and badges.

Reputation

Reputation unlocks heroic dungeons, your head and shoulder enchants, and key items. Grind these early to bring your gear online.

Caster Priority Reputations

  • Keepers of Time (Revered) for Glyph of Power, your head enchant, and the CoT heroic key.
  • The Sha'tar, Lower City, Cenarion Expedition, Honor Hold / Thrallmar (Revered) for their heroic keys.
  • The Violet Eye (Karazhan) for Violet Signet of the Archmage, a top caster ring; wear the lower ranks while grinding.
  • The Consortium and The Scale of the Sands (later) for rings and trinkets.

Aldor vs Scryers

Pick one Shattrath faction (mutually exclusive). For a Frost Mage it mainly sets your shoulder enchant:

  • Scryers: Greater Inscription of the Orb (+12 spell dmg, +15 crit), the higher-crit option that pairs well with Shatter.
  • Aldor: Greater Inscription of Discipline (+18 spell dmg, +10 crit), slightly more raw damage.

Both are close; pick whichever matches your guild, trinket plans, and what your other casters are running.
